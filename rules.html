Field Manual

RULES &
REGULATIONS

Violation of any protocol results in immediate disqualification. The organizing committee reserves absolute authority.

01

ELIGIBILITY & TEAM COMPOSITION

  • › Open to all UG and PG students from all colleges.
  • › No limit to number of teams per college.
  • › Each team must consist of exactly two (2) participants from the same institution.
02

EQUIPMENT PROTOCOL

CRITICAL: Every participant must carry their own physical calculators.

  • › Strictly prohibited: Mobile phones for calculations during events.
  • › Teams without calculators will be handicapped but not excused from participation.
03

SURPRISE ROUND STRUCTURE

  • › All rounds are surprise rounds.
  • › Format, tasks, and evaluation criteria disclosed only at the start of each round.
  • › Time limits specified at round commencement and strictly enforced.
04

CONDUCT & DISCIPLINE

  • › Strict adherence to instructions from hosts, judges, and coordinators required.
  • › Professional conduct mandatory throughout event duration.
  • › Zero tolerance: Vulgarity, plagiarism, disrespect toward volunteers/coordinators/judges leads to immediate disqualification and further consequences.
05

ORIGINALITY & FAIR PRACTICE

  • › Participants must ensure absolute originality of ideas.
  • › Any form of copying, unfair practice, or academic dishonesty will result in immediate elimination.
06

JUDGING & AUTHORITY

  • › All judging and scoring decisions regarding financial rationale and risk assessment are final.
  • › Judges' decisions are binding. No appeals will be tolerated.
07

LIABILITY & MODIFICATIONS

  • › Organizing committee not responsible for issues arising from technical glitches.
  • › Committee reserves absolute right to modify rules, formats, or schedules at any stage of the competition.

FINAL AUTHORITY

Participation constitutes acceptance of all rules and granting of discretionary powers to the organizing committee. The market is unforgiving. So are we.
